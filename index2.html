Call for Papers

Authors are cordially invited to submit papers through an E-mail: icmseat@gitam.in on or before 1st September 2022. Research contributions are solicited in all areas pertinent to the conference theme and including the following:
  • Abstract Algebra
  • Mathematic
  • Dynamical Systems
  • Dynamics
  • Logic
  • Point Theory
  • Mathematical Modelling
  • Operations Research
  • probability and Statistics
  • Data Analytics
  • Computational Methods
  • Reliability & Stochastic Process
    • Developments in Technology (CSE, Mechanical Engineering) Primarily, the author(s) can submit an unpublished research paper in any format (MS Word/PDF) for the purpose of presentation in the conference. After peer review, selected papers will be published in a SCOPUS indexed journal. The publication charges and other details will be informed to the author(s) after the peer review process.
